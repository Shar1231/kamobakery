Kleicha:

It’s a stuffed pastry, very delicious with coffee and tea. At present we have three types of Kliche: - Date Stuffed. - Coconut stuffed - Walnut stuffed.

Seasame cake:

Kamo bakery is the only bakery in Vancouver and surrounding areas that makes this cake (CHORAK). the seasame cake is a cookie, cooked with sasame seeds.

Nan:

Kamo Baker makes Tandoori bread daily, the most famous bread in the Middle East. It is very delicious with Kabab, Barbecue, Tashrib, and Pacha. Also, you can use it like pizza bread. You can put it in the freezer for more than one month, when you take it out it needs a few seconds in the microwave or oven to get back fresh.
