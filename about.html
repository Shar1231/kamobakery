About Us

Kamo Bakery is a family business with more than thirty years of experience in bread and pastry baking. All our products are handmade and give a natural taste, when you try our products you feel like you made them in your own home. We put quality of products before profit, so we serve our customers with very delicious products. We try our best to offer high service and use (try before you buy) as a principal with our customers. Kamo Bakery makes fresh and hot products daily and has contact with some non-profit organizations to donate the non-fresh products to some low-income families.
